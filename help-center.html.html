Using the Income Tracker

>>Add Your Monthly Income:


# In the Income Tracker section, start by entering your income amount in the provided form and
click "Add Income".
# This amount will update your total income displayed on the tracker.

>>Log and Categorize Expenses:


# In the Expense Tracker area, select or enter a category (e.g., Rent, Groceries, Shopping) and
then enter the expense amount.
# Click "Add Expense" to save it. This will update the expense summary and adjust your remaining balance.

>>Monitor Remaining Balance:


# The Income Tracker automatically calculates your remaining balance by subtracting
total expenses from your income.
# You can view a detailed breakdown of expenses by category and see the remaining
funds after all expenses are accounted for.

>>Visualize Expenses with Charts:


The tracker includes a pie chart of your expenses, making it easy to see how much you
spend in each category at a glance.
